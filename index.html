Let's talk about the simpler, quicker and more convenient way to get tested…

Convenience

With discreetly packaged tests and next day delivery, there's no need to take time off work to visit a doctor or queue in a clinic. Results are processed in our labs and delivered to your online account in 2 to 5 days.

Cost

Getting tested often costs more than just the STI test itself. Our at-home STI tests remove additional (or hidden!) costs like consultation fees, travel costs and time off work, and we can include treatment too.

Confidentiality

Every aspect of the service is 100% private and confidential to protect your identity – from discreet billing and plain packaging for our kits to all test samples and results being anonymised.

We never share your personal information with any third parties or insurance providers.

Accuracy

Our home STI tests use the same laboratories as sexual health clinics, hospitals and state healthcare. We use cutting edge patient ID technology to ensure anonymity and prompt delivery of results.

We’re with you every step of the way

From results through to treatment we’re here to help you.

  • Discreet Packaging & Billing

    We use discreet billing, non branded packaging and anonymised tests, so there's no need to worry.

  • Clinical Accuracy & Guidance

    Our medical team of registered nurses and board-certified physicians review every test to ensure the highest standards of care and advice on treatment.

  • Prescriptions & Treatment

    If you are diagnosed with an STI we can provide you with a prescription and the option of collecting at your local pharmacy.
